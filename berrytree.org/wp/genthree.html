Spouse Information

Mary Berry  1st married about 1777,  2nd marriage about December 1781

Mary Berry Camp died in South Carolina before 1800

Spouse # 1 George Waggoner died in 1781 He was the son of Henry Waggoner

George died from wounds sustained in the Battle of Guilford Courthouse                                He wrote his will 2 weeks after the Battle was fought

Spouse # 2 James Kemp/Camp 1768 – 1833

Robert Berry Jr. married about 1778

Spouse Mary Waggoner daughter of Henry Waggoner

John Berry born about 1762

Married Martha Stepp on September 16 1893 in Person County, NC

Isaac Berry had a daughter by Lidia Berry It is not known if they were married.

Joshua Berry married January 20th 1792

Spouse Nancy Ellison 1775 – cir 1858

Elizabeth Berry

Never Married had a daughter in 1892 (Mary) by William Riley 1892

Thomas Berry married August 12th 1800

Spouse Sarah Cate December 27 1782 – March 7 1875

David J. Berry married February 27th 1797

Spouse Mary Blalock cir 1772 – Uknown

William Berry married August 11th 1799

Spouse Hannah Cate 1770 – February 25 1858

Henry Berry Had a daughter by Fanny Ashley but they were never married. This is known because Fanny Ashley was living with her daughter Mary Ashley Berry King in 1850.

Henry left the Plantation Home he had inherited to his daughter Mary Ashley Berry and her husband Thomas King in 1855
