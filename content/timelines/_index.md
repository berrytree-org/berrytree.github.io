The Berry family timelines trace the journey of Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry's descendants across four centuries of American history. Use the **Wheel of Time** to navigate through the centuries — from the earliest Berry roots in Virginia and North Carolina, through westward expansion into Georgia, Alabama, Tennessee, and Texas, to the present day. Each era tells the story of a family shaped by the events of its time.
