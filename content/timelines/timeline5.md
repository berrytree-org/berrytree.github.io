<strong>1833 </strong>There was a migration of several Berry relatives and families from Orange County North Carolina to Georgia and Alabama this year.

1. Elizabeth Berry Franklin and her husband were in Alabama in 1835 when their son Henry Berry Franklin was born. They returned to Floyd County Georgia by the time the 1840 Floyd County Georgia Census was taken Roll 41 Page 256.

2. John Berry who was born in Orange County NC in 1815 listed in Fayette County Alabama in 1840. He died in Rusk Texas in 1862

3. Robert C Berry( brother or cousin to Thomas Person Berry ?) and Charles Wilson (brother to Felix Wilson) Witnessed this Deed 5.
