Carlton Berry has been difficult to find after the 1920 Census. He was living with his Step mother Elizabeth Staley Berry, his half sister Catherine Burkman Berry, and his brother, Cleo in 1910. According to family members Carlton moved to Tennessee and changed his name to escape a bad relationship with his first wife. Cleo Berry was the only member of the family who knew what Carlton's new name was and Cleo died without revealing his brother's new name to the family. Carlton is reported to have two Sons.
