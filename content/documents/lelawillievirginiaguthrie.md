<strong>Lela Willie Virginia Guthrie </strong>was born on December 26, 1878 in Minden, Rusk County Texas and Died on March 31st 1952 in Henderson, Rusk County Texas. She married Lonzo (Lonnie) Joseph Hunt who was born on February 15th 1878 in Rusk County Texas and died on August 23rd 1958 in Nacigdoches County, Texas. Granny Hunt was blind in her right eye.The picture on the left is Lonzo (Lonnie) Joseph and his wife Lela Guthrie Hunt.
