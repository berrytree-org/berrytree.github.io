Below is a list of items that Robert Berry Sen, and his family bought at William Cate's Estate Sale in 1803.  William and Elizabeth Smith (Messersmitt) Cate were Hannah and Sarah Cates' parents and William and Thomas Berry's father and mother-in-law.

- **Robert Berry Sr.**
  - 3 Cups & Saucers, 0, 3, 0
- **Elizabeth Berry**
  - 1 Keeler, 0, 6, 0
- **Robert Berry Jr.**
  - 1 Frow, 1, 0, 0
  - 1 Hammer, 0, 2, 0
- **Joshua Berry**
  - 1 Cross & round Share, 0, 5, 0
  - 2 Bridles, 0, 2, 0
  - 1 Barrell, 0, 3, 6
  - 1 Table, 0, 2, 6
- **Thomas Berry**
  - 1 Quart, 1, 0, 0
  - 1 Bell, 0, 2, 0
  - 2 Sugar Pots, 0, 1, 6
  - 1 Pair of Cards, 0, 2, 1
- **William Berry**

  - 1 Bed, 0, 5, 10
  - 1 cotton wheel, 0, 0, 5

  - 1 Plough, 0, 15, 0
