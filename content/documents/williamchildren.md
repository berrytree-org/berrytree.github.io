<strong>CHILDREN</strong>
<ul>

	<li><strong><a href="/documents/williamclarenceberrync/">William Clarence Berry </a></strong> was born October 17 1802 in Orange County NC and died in Burnet County Texas on September 8th 1878 at the age of 76. William married Sally Bowles in Orange County NC on November 20th 1826. He married his 2nd wife Massa Ann Thompkins in Fayette County Alabama about 1836.</li>
	<li><strong><a href="/ancestors/robertcberryalabama/">Robert C Berry </a></strong> was born on April 14th 1805 and died in Berry Alabama, Fayette County in 1888 at the age of 83. Robert C Berry married Sarah Chappell about 1839 in Fayette County Alabama.</li>
	<li><strong> Thomas Person Berry </strong> was born June 1 1808 in Orange County, North Carolina, and died there on April 30th 1884 at the age of 76. Thomas Person Berry married Sarah Lunsford on September 28 1831. He married his 2nd wife, Lucy Brown, on August 24th 1871. Thomas married his 3rd wife, Elizabeth Peed Bowles on December 3rd 1883. All of his marriages were performed in Orange County, North Carolina.</li>
