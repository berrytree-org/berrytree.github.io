# About Us

## Benjamin "Ben" Berry Henderson Sr.

![Ben Henderson](/images/ben-henderson.jpg)

Benjamin "Ben" Berry Henderson Sr. has been researching his maternal Berry family for almost 20 years and has amassed a large database of family member information.

Ben's first self-published book, *Our Berrys in Frontier America*, was published in 2012.

Ben's second book, *Robert Berry Family Tree Index*, is available as a free download from the [Books](/books/) section. We are requesting that you search the book for your ancestors and send us any corrections, additions, or deletions based on your own research.

The third book, *Our Berrys in Frontier America — Revised and Updated*, is a corrected version of the original book with 60 additional pages of information. The book is printed by a professional printer and can be purchased directly from berrytree.org by clicking on [Books](/books/).
