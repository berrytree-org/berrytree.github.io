**MARY LOU TURLEY**, known to family as "Lurie", was born about 1872 in Milton County, Georgia. <!-- UNSOURCED --> Lurie married John Thomas Hartsfield in 1887. <!-- UNSOURCED --> There were three sons born to this union. John Hartsfield died in 1901 and Lurie married John England. <!-- UNSOURCED -->

Mary Lou Turley Hartsfield England died in 1958. She was buried at Harmony Grove Baptist Church Cemetery, in Cobb County Georgia. <!-- UNSOURCED -->
