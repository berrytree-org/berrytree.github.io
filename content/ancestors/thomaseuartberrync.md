<strong>Tho</strong><strong>mas Ewart Berry </strong> was born January 25th 1880 d. December 15,1960. Me married Mary Ella Tilley On November 29 1903. Mary Ella Tilley was born July 25 1881 and died on September 17th 1945. Both are buried at Berrys Grove Baptist Church.Orange, County, NC. Thomas Ewart was called by his middle name by the family. W.P Berry Sr. is on the left Willie Adolphus is in the center, Thomas Ewart Berry is on the right.
