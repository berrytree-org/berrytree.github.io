**LEWIS BERRY** was born on April 27th, 1826 in Gallia Ohio. <!-- UNSOURCED --> Lewis Berry married Eliza Haywood who was also born in Preble County Ohio on November 16, 1833. <!-- UNSOURCED --> Lewis and Eliza were married on March 20th, 1851, Squire Bowen of Thorton, Indiana officiated. Elizabeth Haywood was the daughter of Abijah and Naomi Haywood of Essex County, N.J. Elizabeth's mother died in 1851 and her father Abijah was living with Lewis and Elizabeth in their 1860 and 1870 census listing.

Lewis was listed as a farmer but he shared a common trait with many of the Berry men in the Robert and Elizabeth Cate family. Many of the descendants of this family acquired large plots of land over their lifetime. Lewis was no exception. His estate included many plots of land in Wichita Kansas and was probably valued in the neighborhood of $20,000. At one time he purchased and owned 20 lots in the city of Wichita Kansas. Lewis enjoyed good health and was a very hardworking individual. He maintained a very good standing in the communities in which he resided as well. Lewis lived at 1600 South Mead Avenue in Wichita Kansas. That is still a valid address today in 2008. Lewis died in Wichita Kansas on March 6, 1907 at the age of 81 years. <!-- UNSOURCED --> Lewis was named for his Uncle Lewis Berry who lived in Orange County North Carolina all of his life. <!-- UNSOURCED -->
