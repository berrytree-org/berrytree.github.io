## **The Story of Henry Bradford Berry**

{{< img-float src="/images/henrybradfordberry.jpg" alt="Headstone of Henry Bradford Berry at Pleasant Hill Methodist Church Cemetery" side="left" >}}

{{< img-float src="/images/melissajeffreysberry.jpg" alt="Headstone of Malissa Jane Jeffries Berry at Pleasant Hill Methodist Church Cemetery" >}}

**Henry Bradford Berry** was born Dec 6, 1829 in Fayette County, Alabama. <!-- UNSOURCED --> He married **Malissa Jane Jeffries** on June 4, 1874. <!-- UNSOURCED --> Malissa was born Nov 6, 1851 and died on October 6, 1921. <!-- UNSOURCED --> Her headstone is pictured on the right. Henry Bradford Berry's headstone is on the left. Henry Bradford died on Apr 22, 1895. <!-- UNSOURCED --> Henry and Malissa, along with his parents, Thompson and Rosanah were buried in Pleasant Hill Methodist Church Cemetery in Fayette County, Alabama.

Henry Bradford and Malissa Berry had 6 children: Clara Mae, Henry Alberta, James Marvin, Cordelia Frances, Mattie Lee, and William Thomas. I will send you copies of the oil company's descendant chart and the hand written probate record for Malissa. Apparently Henry died without a will and Malissa needed to get ownership of the property. Malissa was 21 years younger than Henry according to the 1880 census.

In the 1920 Census Malissa J Berry's grandson, Henry D. Strickland was living with her.
