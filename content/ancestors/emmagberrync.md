<strong>Generation 6 EMMA G. BERRY</strong> was born 18th of November 1866 in Orange County NC. She was the oldest child of John Robert Berry and his wife Elizabeth Frances Bowling.
Emma G. Berry was 18 years old when she married John Sanders Berry on October 15th 1884. Emma Greenwood Berry died in Orange County, North Carolin,a on the 12th  February 1945.

John Sanders Berry descends from Joshua Berry.
Emma G. Berry descends from William Berry.
Joshua Berry and William Berry were brothers.
Emma nd John lived in Person County North Carolina.
My mother always said the the two Berry Families were unrelated and I am sure she beleived this. It was not until after my Mothers death that I discovered the two families had comon ancestors in Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry. This couple had eight children.

Emma's Headstone list her birth date as November 18th 1866 and death on Feb 12 1945.

Emma's Death Certificate list her birth date as November 1 1867 and death on Feb 12 1946.

My Personal Records list her birth date as October 31st 1866 and death on Feb 12 1945.
