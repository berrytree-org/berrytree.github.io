## **The Story of Robert J. Berry**

Robert J. Berry was born about 1867 in Georgia. <!-- UNSOURCED --> Robert and his brother were killed in a car train accident in 1921. Robert was manager of a saw mill and was not married. His middle initial may be an (I) instead of (J). It is recorded both ways.

**VIEW** Robert Berry's Death Certificate

- Robert Berry b. 1858, d. 1921 in a car [train accident Newspaper Clipping](/images/berrybrothers.pdf)

Robert J. Berry was the son of Joshua and the grandson of [David J. Berry](/ancestors/david-j-berry-sr/).
