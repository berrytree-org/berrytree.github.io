## **Ada Lee Ann Berry Rogers**

**Ada Lee Berry** was born July 11th 1875. <!-- UNSOURCED --> On Sept 1, 1895 she married William Jackson Rogers. <!-- UNSOURCED --> William J. Rogers was born in Granville County, NC on October 10th 1864. <!-- UNSOURCED --> He was the son {{< img-float src="/images/jrberrychildren640large.jpg" alt="John Robert Berry's children" side="left" >}} of William Clinton Rogers and Rachel **Starke** Holeman.

We need to look at the Starke Surname of Rachel **Starke** Holeman since three of [David J. Berry's](/ancestors/david-j-berry-sr/) descendants in Georgia married into the Starke Family. This is the first time I have seen the name in Orange County, North Carolina.

Ada Lee Ann Berry Rogers died on November 22nd 1968 and William Jackson Rogers died on April 12th 1931. <!-- UNSOURCED --> They are both buried at Berry's Grove Baptist Church. **To View Newspaper Clipping Click on the Small Image.** William Jackson and Ada Lee Ann Berry Rogers had nine children.
