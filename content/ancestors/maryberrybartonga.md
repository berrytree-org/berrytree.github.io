<h2>Mary Berry is the daughter of David J. and Mary Blalock Berry</h2>
ANCESTORS
<ol>
	<li>Gen Robert and Mary Williamson Berry. Princess Anne County, Virginia.</li>
	<li>Gen Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry, Orange County, North Carolina.</li>
	<li>Gen  David J. and Mary Blalock Berry, NC and GA.</li>
</ol>
<strong>4. Gen Mary Berry was born in 1806 in Orange County North Carolina. She married Thomas Howard Barton on February 24, 1831 in Fayette County Georgia.</strong>

<a href="/images/david-j-berrys-daughter-mary.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8800" src="/images/david-j-berrys-daughter-mary.jpg" alt="david j berrys daughter mary" width="739" height="509" /></a>

 

Thomas H. Barton Who married Mary Berry was  born  in Orange County North Carolina in 1811.

<strong>Thomas H. Barton most likely migrated to Georgia in the same Wagon train that Mary Berry and her family were in
Mary Berry Barton and Thomas Howard Barton are both buried at Tanners Baptist Church Cemetery, Clayton County Georgia.</strong>
