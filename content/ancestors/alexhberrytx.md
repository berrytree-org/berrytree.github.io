{{< img-float src="/images/alexhjosieberry-e1421577909136.jpg" alt="Alexander Howard Berry and Josephine Priscilla Gregory Berry" side="left" >}}**Alexander Howard Berry** was born on August 24th 1854 in New Danville, Rusk County Texas. <!-- UNSOURCED -->

On April 17th 1877 Alexander married Josephine Priscilla Gregory in Henrietta, Clay County Texas. <!-- UNSOURCED -->

Josephine P. Gregory was born on July 7th 1858. <!-- UNSOURCED -->
