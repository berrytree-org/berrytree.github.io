<h2>Winifred Berry daughter of David J. and Mary Blalock Berry</h2>
ANCESTORS

Generation 1 Robert and Mary Williamson Berry

Generation 1 Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry

Generation 2 David J. and Mary Blalock Berry

<strong>Winifred Berry</strong> is the daughter of David J. Berry and Mary Blalock. She married Joshua Norman in Orange County, North Carolina on September 19 1820. Joshua was born in North Carolina in 1790 and died .  Joshua Norman died on November 13 1832 in Henry County Georgia. Joshua Norman was a very young man when he died and the couple only had two children as far as we know at the time of this writing. Joshua Norman and his family probably went to Georgia sometine around 1828 when David J. Berry and the rest of his family did. David J. Berry and Joshua Norman Listed in 1830 Georgia census. Winifred Norman was living with her son, David Norman in the 1850 and 1860 Georgia Census' Source Citation: Year: 1850; Census Place: District 29, Fayette, Georgia; Roll: M432_69; Page: 42; Image: 88.
Source Citation: Year: 1860; Census Place: Northen District, Clayton, Georgia; Roll: M653_117; Page: 102; Image: 103.
