## **Nancy Berry, daughter of Joshua and Nancy Ellison Berry**

### **Ancestors**

1. Generation Robert and Mary Williamson Berry: Princess Anne County, Virginia
2. Generation [Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry](/ancestors/robert-berry-oc/): Orange County, North Carolina.
3. Generation [Joshua and Nancy Ellison Berry](/ancestors/joshua-berry-sr/): Orange County, North Carolina.

4. Nancy Berry born 1810 and died in March 1883: Orange County, North Carolina. <!-- UNSOURCED --> Nancy Berry was never married and died with leaving a will. Nancy Berry's nephew Eli D. Berry was appointed Administrator of Nancy Berry's Estate.
