## **The Story of Franklin Pierce Berry**

{{< img-float src="/images/frankpberryky.jpg" alt="Portrait of Franklin Pierce Berry" side="left" >}}

**Franklin Pierce Berry** was born on July 12th, 1854 in Uniontown Kentucky and died on January 1st, 1909 in Williamson Co., Illinois. <!-- UNSOURCED -->
On February 6th, 1878 Franklin P. Berry married **Sophia M. Sellers** in Evansville, Indiana. Sophie was the daughter of Orman Sellers and Louisa Ashby. She was born on April 6th, 1860 in Anton, Kentucky, and died in 1912. <!-- UNSOURCED -->

{{< img-float src="/images/sophiesellersberryky.jpg" alt="Portrait of Sophie Sellers Berry, wife of Franklin Pierce Berry" side="left" >}}

Franklin Pierce Berry was most likely named after the President of the United States The fourteenth President of the United States was Franklin Pierce and he served in that office from 1853 until 1857. This coincides with the birth of Franklin Pierce Berry. President Pierce was a Democrat born in the North but had Southern sympathies.
