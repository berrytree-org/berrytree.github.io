<strong>Robert Berry (OC)</strong> and <strong>Elizabeth Cate</strong> named their first daughter <strong>Mary Berry</strong> and their first son <strong>Robert Berry Jr.</strong>
Using the English naming pattern the first son would be named after his Paternal grandfather the first daughter would be named after the Paternal grandmother. <strong>Robert Berry (OC)</strong> parents were <strong>Robert</strong> and <strong>Mary Williamson Berry (PAC)</strong>

Using the same naming pattern one would believe that <strong>Robert Berry's (PAC)</strong> father might be <strong>Richard Berry</strong> because
they named their first son <strong>Richard Berry. Robert</strong> and <strong>Mary Williamson Berry</strong> only had two sons.

<strong>Robert Berry (OC)</strong> was just a baby when his father died in 1730.
