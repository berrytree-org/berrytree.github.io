## **William Aaron Turley, son of Mary J. Berry and Nathan Turley**

### **Ancestors**

- Generation Robert and Mary Williamson Berry: Princess Anne County, Virginia.
- Generation [Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry](/ancestors/robert-berry-oc/): Orange County, North Carolina
- Generation [David J. and Mary Blalock Berry](/ancestors/david-j-berry-sr/): NC and Georgia
- Generation Robert and Elizabeth Everitt Berry: Georgia
- Generation Mary Jori Berry and Nathan Turley: Alabama

**WILLIAM AARON TURLEY** born August 26, 1879 and died November 6, 1960. <!-- UNSOURCED --> William Aaron Turley was the youngest child of Nathan and Mary J. Berry Turley. He was born on August 26th, 1876 according to his tombstone, however on the 1880 census he is listed as six months old. Since he left home at an early age he may have added three years to his age. <!-- UNSOURCED -->

![Nathaniel Arie Turley and William Aaron Turley, early 1920s](/images/natandwilliamturleygasmall.jpg)

It is not known exactly where he wandered before he finally settled in Alabama about 1908. The picture on the right is of [Nathaniel Arie](/ancestors/nathanielarieturleyga/) (on the left) and William Aaron Turley on the right. Nathaniel Arie Turley is Will's brother. This picture was taken in the early 1920's. We know that William lived both in Blount and Morgan Counties Alabama before he married Emma Allena Berry. Emma who is the daughter of Robert Riley and Martha "Mattie" Stark Berry. Will, as he was known in Alabama, and Emma Turley had four children. Melvin Ray Berry died at birth. <!-- UNSOURCED -->

[![Will and Emma Turley with Mattie Stark Berry and children](/images/fred-turley-martha-mattie-starke.jpg)](/images/fred-turley-martha-mattie-starke.jpg)

In the picture shown above, Will and Emma B. Turley are with Will's mother and their young family. Standing in the back: Emma and Will Turley. Seated is Mattie Stark Berry, Emma and Will's Grandmother. Mattie Berry is holding her granddaughter Mattie Gertrude Turley and standing is Fred Turley.

**Children:**

- Fred Ernest Turley b March 13, 1913 d March 13, 1978
- Mattie Gertrude Turley

Mattie Gertrude Turley was the second child of Will and Emma. She married J. B. Powell, son of John David and Nancy Smith Powell. They had four children. The oldest, Melvin Ray, died at birth. Lois, Hershal, and Irene are still living. <!-- UNSOURCED -->

**Mary J. Berry and Nathan Turley lived between Marietta and Roswell Georgia.**
