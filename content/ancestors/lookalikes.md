There have been some interesting developments resulting from our Berry DNA project. These photographs show remarkable physical similarities between Berry descendants from entirely different family lines.

## **Photo #2 — Thomas Berry Line & David J. Berry Line**

<div class="photo-feature">
<a href="/images/rayberry.jpg"><img src="/images/rayberry.jpg" alt="Ernest John Berry and Raymond Berry" /></a>

## **Photo #4 — Robert Berry Jr. Line & William Berry Line**

<div class="photo-feature">
<a href="/images/fmwpberry.jpg"><img src="/images/fmwpberry.jpg" alt="Francis Marion Berry and Wiley P. Berry" /></a>
