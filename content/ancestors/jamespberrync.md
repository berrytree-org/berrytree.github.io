<h2>James P. Berry is the son 0f Thomas Person and Sarah Lunsford Berry</h2>
ANCESTORS

Generation 1 Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry

Generation 2 William and Hannah Cate Berry

Generation 3 Thomas Person and Sarah Lunsford Berry

<strong>JAMES P. BERRY</strong> was born in 1845 in Orange County, North Carolina. On September 11 1871 James P. Berry married Artillia Wedding who was born about 1851. Artillia is the daughter of Joseph and Louisa Burton Wedding. In the 1850 Census Joseph Wedding listed house # 1736 and <img class="alignleft" src="/images/jamesberryncsmall.jpg" alt="annibellegrave" /> <img class="alignright" src="/images/anniebellberryncsmall.jpg" alt="annibellegrave" /> James P. Berry age 8 was living with his father in the house listing # 1747. James and Artillia Berry had six children. Two of them died young.
James Berry was 2 years old when he died. The Headstone on the right is his. Annie Belle Berry was 13 years old when she died. His headstone is on the left on is hers. This is the way the graves are located in the Fiddleton Grave yard where they are buried

 

James P. Berry died on February 15th 1912 at the age of 67.
