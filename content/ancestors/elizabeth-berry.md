## What Is Known About Elizabeth Berry

Elizabeth Berry was born about 1770 in Orange County, North Carolina, one of the ten children of Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry. She never married.

Elizabeth had a daughter, **Mary**, by **William Riley** in approximately 1792. In her father Robert Berry's will, written in 1812, Elizabeth was listed but her circumstances were not detailed.

Very little else has been documented about Elizabeth's life. Further research is needed.
