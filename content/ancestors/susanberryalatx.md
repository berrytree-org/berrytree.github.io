<h2>Susan Berry, Daughter Of George Berry And Susan Berry</h2>
ANCESTORS
<ol>
	<li>Generation  Robert and Williamson Berry, Princess anne County, Virginia.</li>
	<li>Generation  Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry, Orange County, North Carolina.</li>
	<li>Generation  Robert Jr and Mary Waggoner Berry NC & Fayette County, Alabama</li>
	<li>Generation  George and Susan Berry Fayette County, Alabama.</li>
</ol>
<strong>SUSAN BERRY</strong> was born in 1834 in Alabama.
Susan Berry was probably 15 years old when she married married Moses Ray.
They were most likely married in January 1849.
Their first daughter Sarah E Ray was born in November 1849.
Sarah E. Ray was listed as eleven months old in October 1850.
Susan and Moses had six children. Susan died after 1880 in Gregg Texas.
