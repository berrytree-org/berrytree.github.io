**SUSAN BERRY** was born in 1834 in Alabama. <!-- UNSOURCED -->
Susan Berry was probably 15 years old when she married Moses Ray.
They were most likely married in January 1849. <!-- UNSOURCED -->
Their first daughter Sarah E Ray was born in November 1849. <!-- UNSOURCED -->
Sarah E. Ray was listed as eleven months old in October 1850.
Susan and Moses had six children. Susan died after 1880 in Gregg Texas. <!-- UNSOURCED -->
