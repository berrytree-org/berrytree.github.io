## **The Story of Kate Berry**

{{< img-float src="/images/benkateberrymoakeillsmall.jpg" alt="Portrait of Benjamin Franklin Moake and Kate Berry Moake" side="left" >}}

**Kate Berry** Was born Aug 12, 1896 Sebree, Webster Co., Ky, she died Aug 22, 1966 and is buried at Lake Creek Cemetery, Williamson Co., Illinois. <!-- UNSOURCED --> Kate married **Benjamin Franklin Moake** in Williamson Co, Illinois, son of Eziekel Moake and Dell Everett. He was born August 18, 1892 in Lake Creek, Williamson Co., Illinois, and died February 09, 1979 in Williamson Co., Illinois. <!-- UNSOURCED --> In 1941, Kate had her birth certificate replaced, the witnesses for her were A.M. Sellers, an uncle age 75, Samuel Hansford husband of Rebecca Sellers and G.W. Bronson, a non relative age 80 years. The certificate is listed as #175914 in the bureau of vital statistics, Commonwealth of Kentucky, Webster Co.
