## **The Story of Robert Riley Berry**

{{< img-float src="/images/robert-riley-berry.jpg" alt="Photograph of Robert Riley Berry" caption="Robert Riley Berry" >}}

{{< img-float src="/images/mattie_starke_berry-1.jpg" alt="Photograph of Martha Mattie Stark Berry" caption="Martha &quot;Mattie&quot; Stark" >}}

Robert Riley Berry was born June 10, 1840 in Fayette County, Georgia. <!-- UNSOURCED --> **[View Robert's birthday entry in the family bible page](/images/robert-and-eliza-everetts-bible-page.jpg)**. Robert Riley Berry grew up in the Roswell, Georgia area of Milton County, Georgia. In 1930 Milton County, Georgia was renamed Fulton County, Georgia. Robert Riley and two of his brothers married three sisters. The young ladies were the daughters of Jonas and Averella Kent Stark. Robert Riley married Martha "Mattie" Stark. **The picture on the left is of Robert Riley and Mattie Stark Berry. Click on the picture to view a larger image.** The other two brothers were William H. Berry who married Matilda Stark and [John Allmon Berry](/ancestors/johnallmonberryga/) who married Sarah "Sally" Stark. Robert died on March 31, 1909 in Vinemont, Cullman County, Alabama, USA[efn_note][Find A Grave profile for **Robert Riley Berry**](https://www.findagrave.com/memorial/71518968/robert-riley-berry)[/efn_note].

Below is a letter that was sent on April 30, 1897 to Robert Riley Berry by one of his brothers, telling him of their mother's death (Eliza Everette Berry).
