## **1800 Orange County, North Carolina Census.**

[![1800 Orange County NC Census showing Berry family entries](/images/djb1800.jpg)](/images/djb1800.jpg)[![1800 Thomas Berry Census entry](/images/tberry1800.jpg)](/images/tberry1800.jpg)

1800 Orange County, North Carolina, listing for [Robert Berry Jr.](/ancestors/robert-berry-jr/), [Robert Berry Sr.](/ancestors/robert-berry-oc/), [Joshua Berry Sr.](/ancestors/joshua-berry-sr/), David J. Berry Sr., William Berry, and [Thomas Berry](/ancestors/thomas-berry/).

William Berry never listed in another Orange County, North Carolina Census. <!-- UNSOURCED -->

**This was the only US Census. in which Thomas Berry ever listed**. We know Thomas was not in Orange County, North Carolina in 1810 because his wife, Sarah Cate Berry, was enumerated in her sister's, [Hannah Cate Berry's](/ancestors/hannahcateberry/) 1810 Orange County, North Carolina Census. There were only 4 males under 10 years old listed in that census.
