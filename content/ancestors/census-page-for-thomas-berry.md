1800 Orange County, North Carolina, listing for [Robert Berry Sr.](/ancestors/robert-berry-oc/), [Robert Berry Jr.](/ancestors/robert-berry-jr/), [Joshua Berry Sr.](/ancestors/joshua-berry-sr/), David J. Berry Sr., William Berry, and [Thomas Berry](/ancestors/thomas-berry/).

William Berry never listed in another Orange County, North Carolina Census. <!-- UNSOURCED -->

**This was the only US Census in which Thomas Berry ever listed.** We know Thomas was not in Orange County, North Carolina in 1810 because his wife, Sarah Cate Berry, was enumerated in her sister's, [Hannah Cate Berry's](/ancestors/hannahcateberry/) 1810 Orange County, North Carolina Census. There were only 4 males under 10 years old listed in that census.
