<strong>ROBERT BERRY</strong> was born on August 2, 1812 in Orange County North Carolina was named for his grandfather Robert Berry who owned the Fiddleton Plantation. He was about sixteen years old when his father David J. Berry decided to remove the family to Georgia. Robert had an Uncle and some Berry Cousins in Fayette County Alabama, Wayne County Tennessee, and some Camp cousins in Georgia. David and his family apparently chose to settle near his sister, Mary Berry Camp's children, in Fayette County Georgia.

Eliza Everett was born on May 30th 1814 in Georgia and died on April 23rd 1891 in Milton County Georgia. Robert was about thirty one years old when he married Eliza Everett on the 16th of June 1833. Eliza Everette was about 29 years old when she married.

Robert & Eliza Everette Berry's Children

Locating the bible page on the left was a very valuable document to find. It list Robert Berry's birth date, his wife Eliza Berry, all of their children,  their birthdates, and some deaths.

Robert and Eliza Everett Berry were in Fayette County Georgia in 1840. The 1840 census for that county shows that they had three boys and 2 girls. This information checks out to what is known about their family.
