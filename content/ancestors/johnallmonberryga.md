## **The Story of John Allmon Berry**

{{< img-float src="/images/johnallmonberryhomegasmall.jpg" alt="John Allmon Berry's home in Milton County, Georgia, circa 1900" side="right" >}}

John Allmon Berry born July 16th, 1843 in Fayette County, Georgia. <!-- UNSOURCED --> He married Sarah Elizabeth (Sally) Starke. <!-- UNSOURCED --> They had seven children.

This is John Allmon Berry's home about 1900. The picture was taken after their son, Charles E. Berry, had married. They may be the young couple standing on the left. This might have been the family wedding picture taken at the time of their wedding. This home-place was located just outside of Roswell in Milton County, Georgia. In 1930 Milton County, Georgia was renamed Fulton County, Georgia. John Allmon and two of his brothers married three sisters. The young brides were the daughters of Jonas and Averella Kent Stark. John Allmon Berry married Sarah "Sally" Stark, [Robert Riley Berry](/ancestors/robertrileyberryga/) married Martha "Mattie" Stark, and Matilda Stark was William H. Berry's first wife.
