### **Maternal Grandparents**

[Sarah Cate](/ancestors/sarah-cate/) (Daughter of William & Elizabeth Messersmith Cate)

{{< img-float src="/images/david2berry.jpg" alt="Portrait of David Berry, son of Thomas Berry" >}}

**David Berry** was born in Orange County North Carolina in 1802. In 1803 David and his sister, **Eleanor Berry** were very young when their grandfather William Cate died. The picture on the right is David Berry, son of [Thomas Berry](/ancestors/thomas-berry/) born in 1800 died in 1882. In 1803 there was a sale of William Cate's property. [David's father](/documents/william-cates-estate-sale/) purchased several items at his grandfathers William Cate's estate sale in 1803. Three years later on October the 4th 1806 David's Father sold two hundred acres of land to James Miller for one hundred and twenty five pounds ([View here](/ancestors/thomas-berry-land-sale/)). Soon after that sale [Thomas Berry](/ancestors/thomas-berry/) went in search of land in Tennessee. David and his sister never saw their father again. David and his mother and sister lived in Orange County with his Aunt Hannah Berry until he was about 13 years old. Hannah Cate Berry and [Sarah Cate](/ancestors/sarah-cate/) Berry were sisters making their children Double First Cousins. His mother [Sarah Cate](/ancestors/sarah-cate/) Berry remarried on December 11 1815 to John Pigg. They Migrated to Wayne County Tennessee where David grew into manhood. David married **Elizabeth Shipman** about 1826 in Tennessee. <!-- UNSOURCED -->
