John Berry was born in Orange or Person County, North Carolina in 1815. We are positive that he is a descendant of [Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry](/ancestors/robert-berry-oc/) because one of his descendant's Y-DNA matches the Y-DNA of 13 other known Orange County Berrys. He was first discovered living in Rusk County Texas in the 1860 census but later we found him living in Fayette County, Alabama in 1840. John met and married his wife, Mary Howell in Fayette County, Alabama. They had four children born in Alabama, two daughters and two sons. We have a lot of information on the oldest daughter [Sarah Berry](/ancestors/sarahberrytx/) but have not located the second daughter. The rest of their children were born in Texas. We have never found an 1850 Census for John and Mary Howell Berry.
