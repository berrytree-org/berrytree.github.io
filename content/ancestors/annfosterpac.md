<h2>Ann Foster from England and Princess Ann County Virginia</h2>
<h3><strong>Ancestors:</strong></h3>
<a> Parents Unknown Ann Foster and Roger Williamson </a>

<strong>Ann Foster married Roger Williamson</strong>
Ann Foster was born in England in 1620 and died in Princess Ann County Virginia around 1692. Roger died about 1650 and Ann Foster Williamson remarried. Her new husband was John Carraway. Recorded in Virginia Colonial Abstracts, Vol. 31, Lower Norfolk County, Page 19. and she had children by him also.
<h3><strong>Children of Ann and Roger:</strong></h3>
<ul>
	<li>John Williamson</li>
	<li>Bartholomew Williamson</li>
	<li><a href="/documents/richardwilliamsonpac/">Richard Williamson </a>married Mary Kempe</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Children of Ann Foster and John Carraway:</strong></h3>
<ul>
	<li>Mary Carraway married Lancaster Lovett</li>
	<li>Elizabeth Carraway married Nichols</li>
	<li>John Carraway married Ann Kempe</li>
</ul>
Since John Carraways half brother Richard Williamson married Mary Kempe and John Carraway married Ann Kempe. I think that Ann Kempe and Mary Kempe were sisters and both girls would be the daughters of George and Mary Hutchison Kempe
