<h2>David Middleton Berry, Son of Robert Berry Jr. and Mary Camp Berry</h2>
ANCESTORS
<ol>
	<li>Generation Robert and Mary Williamson Berry: Princess Anne County, Virginia</li>
	<li>Generation Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry: Orange County, North Carolina.</li>
	<li>Generation Robert Jr and Mary Waggoner Berry: NC & Fayette County, Alabama</li>
</ol>
<strong>4. Generation DAVID MIDDLETON BERRY</strong> was born April 14th 1805 in Orange County North Carolina He died on May 7th 1880. David married Didama Box about 1826.Didama Box was born in South Carolina on February 14th 1807. She died on December 14th, 1856 in Fayette County Alabama.David Middleton Berry married his second wife, Frances E. Henderson on April 15 1857 in Fayette County Alabama.Frances was born on January 10th 1818 in South Carolina and died May 17th 1885.
