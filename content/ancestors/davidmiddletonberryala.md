## **The Story of David Middleton Berry**

**David Middleton Berry** was born April 14th, 1805 in Orange County, North Carolina. <!-- UNSOURCED --> He died on May 7th, 1880. <!-- UNSOURCED --> David married **Didama Box** about 1826. <!-- UNSOURCED --> Didama Box was born in South Carolina on February 14th, 1807. She died on December 14th, 1856 in Fayette County, Alabama. <!-- UNSOURCED --> David Middleton Berry married his second wife, **Frances E. Henderson** on April 15, 1857 in Fayette County, Alabama. <!-- UNSOURCED --> Frances was born on January 10th, 1818 in South Carolina and died May 17th, 1885. <!-- UNSOURCED -->
