**WILLIAM H BERRY** was born in 1836. He grew up in Orange County and married the girl next door. Sarah Frances King the daughter of Thomas and Mary King. They were married on August 6th 1857. There is much more to the story than this. Sarah Francis King's mother was the granddaughter of **Henry Berry** and William H. Berry was the grandson of **William Berry**. The relationship of the bride and groom would be first cousins once removed. This relationship was not known until Sarah Francis King's sister Susan Ann King Crabtree died in 1920. On her death certificate Susan's husband noted that Susan's mother mother's  name was Mary Berry.
