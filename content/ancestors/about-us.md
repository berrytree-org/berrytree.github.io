<p><img class=" wp-image-1488 size-medium alignright" src="/images/ben_henderson1.jpg" alt="Ben_Henderson1" width="300" height="225"><strong>Benjamin "Ben" Berry Henderson Sr.</strong> has been researching his maternal Berry family for almost 20 years and has amassed a large database of family member information. Ben's first self-published book, "<a href="/books/books/"><strong>Our Berrys in Frontier America</strong></a>" was published in 2012.</p>
<p>Ben's second book  "<a href="/books/books/"><strong>Robert Berry family tree index</strong></a>" is available as a free download from the Books dropdown bar.  We are requesting that you search the book for your ancestors and send us any corrections, additions or deletions based in you own research.</p>
<p><a href="/images/front_page_art.jpg"><img class="wp-image-2912 alignleft" src="/images/front_page_art.jpg" alt="front_page_art" width="250" height="324"></a>The third book "<strong><a href="/books/books/">Our Berrys in Frontier America Revised and Updated</a>" </strong>is a corrected version of the original book with 60 additional pages of information. The book is printed by a professional printer and can be purchased directly from berrytree.org by clicking on Books in the dropdown bar.</p>
