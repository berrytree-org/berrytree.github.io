Addie Lena Berry was born on November 1st 1884 in Alabama. She married Hampton Fantom Maners Sr. on August 26th 1906. <!-- UNSOURCED --> The couple raised four sons and Addie Lena Berry Maners died on September 7th 1975 in South Carolina. <!-- UNSOURCED --> She and her husband are buried in the Floral Garden Memorial Park in High Point, Guilford County, North Carolina.
