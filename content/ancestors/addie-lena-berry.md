Herman Fantom Maners was born 0n June 5th 1886 and died on June 2nd 1967. After her husband died, Addie Lena Maners went to South Carolina to live with her son Jesse Adolphus Maners.

Herman Fantom Maners was the son of William Thomas and Louisa E. Kelly Manners

<a href="/images/H-F-Maners-Grave.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5486" src="/images/H-F-Maners-Grave.jpg" alt="H F Maners Grave" width="714" height="787" /></a>

Addie Lena Berry was born on November 1st 1884 0n Alabama. She married Herman Fanton Maners Sr. on August 26th 1906. The couple raised four sons and Addie Lena Berry Maners died on September 7th 1975 in South Carolina. She and her husband are buried in the Floral Garden Memorial Park in High Point, Guilford County, North Carolina.
