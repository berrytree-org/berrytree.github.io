## **Thomas Young Berry, son of Wiley P. Berry Sr.**

**Ancestors:**

- **Gen 1** — Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry
- **Gen 2** — William and Hannah Cate Berry
- **Gen 3** — Thomas Person and Sarah Lunsford Berry
- **Gen 4** — John Robert and Elizabeth Bowling Berry
- **Gen 5** — [Wiley P.](/ancestors/wiley-p-berry/) and Ida Ann Wilson Berry

{{< img-float src="/images/youngthomasyberrync.jpg" alt="Thomas Young Berry" side="right" >}}

**Thomas Young Berry** was born on May 11, 1903 and died on June 8, 1925. Thomas was the third child of [Wiley P.](/ancestors/wiley-p-berry/) and Ida Ann Wilson Berry. He was five years older than [Sally Malena](/ancestors/sallymalenaberrync/) and apparently would spend time with her on a social level. Malena was always very fond of him.

{{< img-float src="/images/hattiedavisnc.jpg" alt="Hattie Davis" side="right" >}}

Thomas was very popular with the young ladies and dated quite a few of them. One particular young lady managed to catch his fancy more than the others, as it so often happens. **Hattie Davis** and Thomas had decided to get married and had set the date for June 30, 1925.

{{< img-float src="/images/thomasyberrync.jpg" alt="Thomas Young Berry" side="left" >}}

Thomas contracted typhoid fever in the spring of 1925. The family thinks he drank water from a stump hole that was contaminated. Thomas Young Berry died 22 days before he was to be married, on June 8, 1925.

{{< img-float src="/images/vertyoungtyberrync.jpg" alt="Thomas Young Berry as a young student at Meridith School" side="right" >}}

The picture on the right is Thomas Y. Berry in school at Meridith School.

**Children:** Thomas had no children and was never married.

### **Census Information**
