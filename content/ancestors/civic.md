<h2>Robert Berry was a very civic minded citizen</h2>
<h3>OCTOBER, 19 2007</h3>
The November 1777 court session ordered that the following persons Work on the road under Thomas Rountree To Wit: James Allison, William McKee, James Murdock, Moses Gwin, John Murdock Sen, John Wilson, Archd Wilson, William Wilson, William Robinson, William Armstrong, <strong>Robert Berry,</strong> Michl, Robinson & Danl, McMhan. <strong>These men were all neighbors and friends of our Robbert Berry.</strong>

1778 February term Orange NC Court Minutes: The Grand Jury towit: Saml, Allen foreman, John Dickey, Robert Anderson, Wm. Comb, Gilbert Strahorn, James Murdock, <strong>Robert Berry</strong>, John Kelly, William Robinson, Hugh Finley, William Armstrong, Benjamin Thompson, Henry Waggoner, & Andrew McBroom being empanelled & Sworn received their charge and withdrew.

1782 May term Orange NC Court Minutes: <strong>Robert Berry</strong> is excused from attending as Juror at this Court, he having shewn sufficient Reasons &c. NOTE: I think he serving in the Continental army at this time.

1784 May term Orange NC Court Minutes: Issd. <strong>Robert Berry</strong> is appointed Overseer of the road in the room of James Murdock

1786 August term Orange NC Court Minutes: Issd. Michael Robinson is appointed Overseer of the road in the room of <strong>Robert BERRY</strong> from Caswell line to the South fork of Little River.

1789 August term Orange NC Court Minutes: <strong>Robert Berry</strong> is appointed overseer of road in the Room of T Bowles.

1791 May term Orange NC Court Minutes: Wm.Jamison vs. <strong>Robert Berry</strong>, Certiorari. Same Jury as to No. [blank] Sworn find Verdict for Plaintiff for L 1:4:9 & cost.

1791 November Term, Orange NC Court Minutes:Book 4 pg 114 Issd. Adminstration of the estate of James waggoner is Granted to Catherine WAGGONER at the same time she entered into Bond With <strong>Robt. Berry Jun.</strong> Secy. in the sum of L 50.

1791 November Term Orange NC Court Minutes:Book 4 pg 115 Tho. Burks Exrs. vs Geo. Doherty & Wm. Mebane: Debt. Jury sworn to Wit: 1. Martin Parmer, 2. Brice Collins, 3. Tho. Brewer, 4. James Murdock, 5. <strong>Robt. Berry</strong>, 6. Jno. G. Rencher, 7. Jno. Armstrong, 8. Henry Terrel, 9. Tho. Connely, 10. Jno. McCollum, 11. Davd. McCauly, 12. Benj. Peelor, find the Deed to be the act & Deed of the Defts, and that the same is not pd. & assess the plaintiffs damages to L 12:4:10 & cost. Stay Ex. til 15 days before next Court.

1791 November Term Orange NC Court Minutes:Book 4 pg 116 The Execution of a Deed from Wm. James and Thomas Rutherford to <strong>Robert Berry</strong> was duly Acknowledge in open Court by Joseph Taylor Esqr. Atto.&c. and Ordered to be Registered,
