<strong>DORA ELLEN TURLEY</strong> born April 11, 1874 in Milton County Georgia and died June 6, 1915 after giving birth to twin daughters. She married George Robert Pace before 1892. Dora Ellen Turley Pace is buried in the Holly Springs Cemetery, Cobb County Georgia. <a href="/ancestors/dora-ellen-turley-home/"><img class="alignright" src="/images/dorapacehomegasmall.jpg" alt="georgeanddoraturleyhomega" /></a>George and Dora Ellen Turley Pace had 11 children but not all of them lived to be adults. <strong>The picture on the right is a picture of the Pace family around 1910 Click on the picture to view a larger image.</strong>
