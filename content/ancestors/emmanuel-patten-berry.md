<strong>GEN 5</strong> Emanuel Patton  and Clara Susan Worsham Berry

<strong>Emanuel Patton Berry</strong> was born March 10, 1849 in Cobb County, Georgia, and died February 03, 1938 in Baldwin County, Georgia. He married <strong>Clara Susan Worsham</strong> on December 28, 1871 in Milledgeville, Georgia.  She was the daughter of <strong>Erbin C. Worsham</strong> and <strong>Martha Verthal B. Cane</strong>.  Clara was born May 03, 1854, and died January 01, 1936. <strong>Erbin C. Worsham</strong> was born in South Carolina  on October 27 1825 and died  Widowed in Rochelle, Wilcox County, Georgia 0n March 30 1919. he is buried in the Sunshine United Methodist Church Cemetery in Round Oak, Jones County, Georgia USA.
