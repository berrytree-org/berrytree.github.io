    William G. Berry

    Son of David J. and Mary Blalock Berry, Orange County, North Carolina.

    William G. Berry is the son of David J. and Mary Blalock Berry#

    ANCESTORS

    Generation 1 Robert and Mary Williamson Berry

    Generation 1 Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry

    Generation 2 David J. and Mary Blalock Berry

    William G. Berry is the son of David J. Berry and Mary Blalock born about 1812/1815 in Orange County, North Carolina. William G. Berry, joined Phillips Legion in Roswell, GA. William G Berry 21st Inf Reg C Phillips Legion Berry William G. C 21 Georgia Infantry. He was a Private promoted to Corporal in the Confederate Army.William G. Berry married Jane Beck who was born about 1817 in North Carolina about 1835 and they had 12 children William died onJuly 3rd 1886in Fulton County Georgia. Jane Beck Berry died before 1870 in Fulton County Georgia.

    Somebody out there must descend from some of these childrens. If anybody has more information abou this family please contact me.
