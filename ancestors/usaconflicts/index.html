    USA Conflicts & Economic Downturns

    Historical context for Berry family events: the wars they fought in and the economic forces that shaped their migrations.

    This page provides a reference timeline of American conflicts and economic downturns that coincided with the lives of Berry family ancestors. Many Berry descendants served in these conflicts, from the American Revolution through the modern era. See the Veterans Page for portraits of Berry family veterans.
