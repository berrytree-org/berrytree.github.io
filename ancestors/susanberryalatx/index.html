    Born

    Explore in the Berry Family Timeline

    SUSAN BERRY was born in 1834 in Alabama. Susan Berry was probably 15 years old when she married Moses Ray. They were most likely married in January 1849. Their first daughter Sarah E Ray was born in November 1849. Sarah E. Ray was listed as eleven months old in October 1850. Susan and Moses had six children. Susan died after 1880 in Gregg Texas.

    Children of Susan Berry and Moses Ray
