    The Story of David Middleton Berry#

    David Middleton Berry was born April 14th, 1805 in Orange County, North Carolina. He died on May 7th, 1880. David married Didama Box about 1826. Didama Box was born in South Carolina on February 14th, 1807. She died on December 14th, 1856 in Fayette County, Alabama. David Middleton Berry married his second wife, Frances E. Henderson on April 15, 1857 in Fayette County, Alabama. Frances was born on January 10th, 1818 in South Carolina and died May 17th, 1885.
