    Mary died on January 3rd 1899 in Rusk County Texas. Joshua Crow was born on September 19th 1830 and he died on his birthday September 19th 1908 in Rusk County Texas. Joshua and Mary Crow are both buried in the Zion Hill Cemetery Rusk County Texas. The picture on the right was taken at the placement of a Confederate Marker on the grave of Cpl. Joshua Crow on September 27, 2008. The Cousin on the left is a 3rd great grandson of Joshua and Mary Crow. and the lady on the right is a 2nd great granddaughter of Joshua and Mary The Picture on the left is of Mary Aliceann Elizabeth Berry and Joshua Crow
