    Thompson Berry, Son of Robert Berry Jr. and Mary Camp Berry#

    Ancestors:#

    1. Generation Robert and Mary Williamson Berry Princess Anne County, Virginia
    2. Generation Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry Orange County, North Carolina
    3. Generation Robert Jr. and Mary Waggoner Berry NC and Fayette County, Alabama

    THOMPSON BERRY was born December 18, 1801 in Orange County, North Carolina and he died in Fayette County, Alabama on January 8, 1871. Thompson Berry married Rosanah Gutman who was born in South Carolina on November 25, 1803 and died on July 17, 1890 in Fayette County, Alabama.

    Thompson Berry was a very prominent businessman and the town of Berry, Alabama was named for him. The pictures on this page were taken on September 10, 2008.

    Standing in the picture on the left are two descendants of Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry from Orange County, North Carolina. The lady in the picture is actually a descendant of George Berry who was Thompson Berry’s brother. The gentleman in this picture descends from William Berry who was Thompson Berry’s uncle. My 2 Great Grandfather, Thomas Person Berry in Orange County, NC was Thompson Berry’s first cousin.

    Thompson Berry was Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry’s grandson.

    In the picture on the right Thompson Berry and his wife’s memorial was placed beside his actual headstone (seen to the right).

    The picture above is land that once belonged to Robert Berry Jr. and his oldest son, George Berry.

    The city of Berry, Alabama was named for Thompson P. Berry, son of Robert and Mary Waggoner Berry.

    Thompson Berry and Rosanah Gutman were probably married in 1824.

    The children listed on this page were all listed in the 1850 Fayette County, Alabama Census.

    Thompson Berry was born in Orange Co., NC and died in Alabama.
