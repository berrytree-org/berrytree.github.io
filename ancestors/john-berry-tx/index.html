    Generation 5 John Berry

    Born
    1815, Orange or Person County, North Carolina
    Died
    c. 1862, Rusk County, Texas
    Married
    Mary Howell (married c. 1838, Fayette County, Alabama)
    Parents
    George Berry and Susanna Aldridge Berry
    Explore in the Berry Family Timeline

    John Berry was born in Orange or Person County, North Carolina in 1815. We are positive that he is a descendant of Robert and Elizabeth Cate Berry because one of his descendant’s Y-DNA matches the Y-DNA of 13 other known Orange County Berrys. He was first discovered living in Rusk County Texas in the 1860 census but later we found him living in Fayette County, Alabama in 1840. John met and married his wife, Mary Howell in Fayette County, Alabama. They had four children born in Alabama, two daughters and two sons. We have a lot of information on the oldest daughter Sarah Berry but have not located the second daughter. The rest of their children were born in Texas. We have never found an 1850 Census for John and Mary Howell Berry.

    Census Information

    John Berry listed in Fayette County Alabama and Rusk County Texas Census. No Census record has been found for the John Berry family in 1850.

    • John Berry was listed on his Father’s 1830 Fayette County Alabama Census.
    • John Berry listed in the 1840 Fayette County Alabama Census.
    • John Berry listed in the 1860 Rusk County Texas Census.
    • John Berry’s Widow Mary Howell Berry listed in the 1870 Rusk County Texas Census.
    • Mary Howell Berry’s Son Alexander Berry was living a few farms away from her in the 1880 Clay County Texas Census.
    • Mary Howell Berry’s Daughter Amanda F. Berry Taylor and her husband John Bascom Taylor were living on the next farm to her in the 1880 Clay County Texas Census.
    • Mary Howell Berry was living with her son Frank in the 1880 Clay County Texas Census.

    John Berry was born in Orange County North Carolina and died in Texas.
