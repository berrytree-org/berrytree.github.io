    Hampton Fantom Maners, Addie Lena Berry Maners - 50th Wedding Anniversary

    Below is the World War One Draft Registration of Hampton Fantom Maners. He lists his wife, Addie Lena Maners, as the nearest living relative.

    Page 2 of the Registration below describing Hampton Fantom Maners as tall, medium build with Blue eyes and brown hair.

    Hampton Fantom Maners was born on June 5th 1886 and died on June 2nd 1967. After her husband died, Addie Lena Maners went to South Carolina to live with her son Jesse Adolphus Maners.

    Hampton Fantom Maners was the son of William Thomas and Louisa E. Kelly Manners

    Addie Lena Berry was born on November 1st 1884 in Alabama. She married Hampton Fantom Maners Sr. on August 26th 1906. The couple raised four sons and Addie Lena Berry Maners died on September 7th 1975 in South Carolina. She and her husband are buried in the Floral Garden Memorial Park in High Point, Guilford County, North Carolina.

    Addie Lena Berry was the daughter of Jesse Lee and Nancy Ann Henry Berry, Georgia and Alabama
