Historical Documents

Families not forgotten. Records that preserve the past.

What you will find here is the paperwork of real lives — wills that name children by name, deeds that mark the land a family cleared, marriage bonds written in a careful hand, and census records that place our people in a particular county on a particular day. This collection spans three centuries and reaches from colonial Virginia through the Carolinas, Georgia, Alabama, Texas, Ohio, and beyond.
